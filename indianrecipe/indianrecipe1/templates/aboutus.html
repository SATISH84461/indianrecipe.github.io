A Few Words About Us

Serving Best Indian Cuisine Since 1984

Satish Restaurant is as varied and diverse as the Indian cultures and languages of this great sub-continent, its flavors are as exotic as its climate and as varied as its people. We are part of a unique collection of some of finest Indian restaurants. We offer enjoyable, memorable dining experiences – delicious Indian food, exciting interiors & stylish hospitality. Unusually, we offer genuine Indian food from the true sources – the gourmet homes & street stalls across India’s many regions – prepared by skilled chefs from those regions. Come for genuine delicious Indian food.
